BTS

BTS, also known as Bangtan Boys or Bulletproof Boyscouts (roughly translated from Korean) is a boy group from South Korea who debuted on July 2013. They have 7 members including: RM, Jin, Suga, J-Hope, Jimin, V, and Jungkook. Hybe label revealed that BTS members would begin their mandatory military service, with plans to reunite in 2025 after completing their terms.

J-Hope Completes His Mandatory Military Service

After being formally released from military service on October 17, 2024 KST, J-hope performed live for supporters on Weverse.

Jin to Release His First Solo Album "Happy"

The release date and track list for Jin's eagerly anticipated first solo album have finally been announced. Happy, the K-pop sensation's first full-length album released outside of the on-hiatus group's circle, is scheduled for release on November 15.
